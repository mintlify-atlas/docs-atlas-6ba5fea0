## Overview

A minimal, professional portfolio template to showcase your skills, work experience, and projects. Perfect for developers looking to build their personal brand and showcase their work.

## Features

Comprehensive sections to tell your professional story:

- **Hero Section**: Introduction with your photo and tagline
- **About Section**: Tell your story and background
- **Work Section**: Showcase your professional experience
- **Education Section**: Display your educational background
- **Projects Section**: Highlight your best work
- **Hackathon Section**: Show your hackathon participation and wins
- **Contact Section**: Make it easy for people to reach you
- **Blog**: Share your thoughts and expertise

## Tech Stack

Built with modern web technologies:

- **Next.js**: React framework for production
- **React**: Modern UI library
- **TypeScript**: Type-safe JavaScript
- **TailwindCSS**: Utility-first CSS framework
- **Motion**: Smooth animations and transitions
- **shadcn/ui**: Beautiful, accessible components

## Getting Started

1. Clone the repository from GitHub
2. Install dependencies with `npm install`
3. Update your information in the config file
4. Add your projects, work experience, and education
5. Customize colors and styling to match your brand
6. Deploy to Vercel for free hosting
